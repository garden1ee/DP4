Welcome to Puddies!

Puddies is a social network service for dog owners. Let your dog make nearby friends and for happier dog life!



Recommend friends you would absolutely like

Our strong friend recommendation connects you with lovely dogs in your neighborhood. You can simply fill out your profile and give your preferences to meet soulmate of your dog.

Join groups with common interests

Looking for frisbee friends? How about sharing hand-made snack recipes? In Puddies, you can find groups of same interests to meet up, do activities together, and share useful tips. Or you can even make your own group!

Be conneted, be safe!

In Puddies, you don't need to leave your contact info. With our strong built-in message service, you can be fully connected with your friends just standing here. Just take a try!
